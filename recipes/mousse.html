Choclate Mousse

Ingredients

  • 150g Dark Chocolate
  • 150g Milk Chocolate
  • 6 Eggs (separated)
  • 6 Tablespoons Caster Sugar
  • 600ml Double Cream
  • 1 Tablespoon instant coffee (dissolved in small amount of water)
  • Splash of Liqueur (coffee based works best, such as Tia Maria)

Method

  1. Melt choclate in bowl over boiling water (don't get water in the choclate!)
  2. Add egg yolks caster sugar, stir until dissolved
  3. Add coffee and liqueur, stir well
  4. Whip cream until thick and fold into mixture
  5. Whip egg whites until stiff and then fold into mixture
  6. leave to set in fridge (It is best to leave overnight)
